### 🎯 1. Executive Vision & Problem Statement
In the South African economy, millions of creditworthy individuals are "Credit Invisible" because they lack traditional credit history. This project demonstrates an end-to-end AI lifecycle that utilizes **Alternative Behavioral Data** to safely and transparently transition these individuals into the formal economy.

### 🚀 3. The 5-Phase Technical Roadmap



#### **Phase 1: Business Understanding**
* Defined a multi-pillar risk strategy based on **Stability, Consistency, Velocity, and Discipline**.
* Set a target success metric of a **Gini Coefficient > 0.45**.

#### **Phase 2: Synthetic Data Design**
* Architected a **10,000-record dataset** simulating real-world alternative data signatures.
* Engineered custom features like the **Volatility Index** to capture spending discipline.

#### **Phase 3: EDA & Executive Visualization (Tableau)**
* Developed an **Executive Risk Overview** dashboard achieving a **77% targeted approval rate**.
* Integrated business-impact subtitles to guide executive decision-making.

#### **Phase 4: Model Training & Performance**
* Trained a high-performance Gradient Boosting model.
* **Final Result:** Achieved a **0.55 Gini Coefficient**, significantly exceeding initial performance targets.

#### **Phase 5: Explainability & Regulatory Ethics**
* Implemented **NCA-compliant Reason Codes** using SHAP Waterfall plots.
* Developed an automated pipeline to provide transparent credit refusals (e.g., "High Spending Volatility").



---

### 📊 4. Key Business Findings
* **Stability is Key:** Long-term digital tenure (SIM tenure) is the single strongest predictor of low default risk.
* **Operational Readiness:** The model generates actionable data tables that bridge the gap between AI code and call-center customer communication.
* **Ethics by Design:** The engine is strictly behavioral, ensuring compliance with **POPIA** and the **National Credit Act (NCA)**.
